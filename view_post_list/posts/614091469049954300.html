組織介紹｜克林頓醫療衛生服務組織

克林頓醫療衛生服務組織（ The Clinton Health Access Initiative, Inc. 簡稱：CHAI）是一家全球性醫療組織，致力於在中低收入國家拯救生命及減輕疾病負擔，以及強化公、私營部門能力，打造高質量衛生系統，直至無需我們的幫助也能自營成功。目前，克林頓醫療衛生服務組織與將近二十五個國家/地區的政府合作，七十多個國家/地區直接受益於克林頓醫療衛生服務組織協商達成的低價藥品，診斷測試和疫苗協議。

職位介紹

克林頓醫療衛生服務組織的疫苗計劃具有一定規模與復雜性，目前正在尋找合格的候選人來管理其撥款、內部運營和財務，以利有效地實現組織使命和計畫目標。錄取後，候選人將與克林頓醫療衛生服務組織的全球疫苗計劃的管理團隊緊密合作。

職責範圍

（A）捐款管理

（B）內部營運管理

（C）疫苗項目財務管理與財務系統加強

（D）管理子團隊的財務與營運

條件要求

  • 相關領域畢業之學士（碩士以上更佳）
  • 擁有七年在步調快速的工作環境之經驗；至少擁有四年相關工作經驗。
  • 經過驗證的團隊管理能力
  • 出色的組織和項目管理能力、解決問題能力、分析能力、精通 Excel
  • 優秀的書寫與交流能力
  • 出色的人際交往能力，能夠在多元文化且偏遠環境裡，高效地進行跨組織工作
  • 熟悉財務系統、預算和管理
  • 認同組織價值觀與使命
  • 能夠根據工作需求到外地出差
  • 可獨立工作、自我激勵以及提出新計畫的能力
  • 能夠在高壓環境中，高效工作，同時靈活處理多項任務

加分條件

  • 能以法語作為工作語言，在職場與生活環境中有效地運用文法及字彙，充分表達自己的意思，而且不會因為語言能力不足造成溝通障礙或理解困難
  • 與撒哈拉沙漠以南的非洲國家有合作經驗
  • 具有開發或強化系統、強化流程、變更管理、轉型營運之能力及經驗
  • 擁有與捐助者溝通和書信往來之能力

Position: Manager, Vaccine Program Operations & Project Management 

location: Rwanda, kigali 

Full time 

Applications open until position filled.

Overview 

The Clinton Health Access Initiative, Inc. (CHAI) is a global health organization committed to saving lives and reducing the burden of disease in low-and middle-income countries, while strengthening the capabilities of governments and the private sector in those countries to create and sustain high-quality health systems that can succeed without our assistance. Currently, CHAI partners with governments in more than 25 countries, while more than 70 countries directly benefit from the low-priced drugs, diagnostic tests, and vaccines agreements that CHAI has negotiated. 

Roles and Responsibilities  

CHAI’s vaccine program has been growing significantly in size and complexity and is seeking a qualified candidate to manage its grant and internal operations as well as its finance, so that the program delivers more effectively on its mission and goals.  The successful candidate, along with a team of two direct reports, will work closely with the management team of CHAI’s global vaccines program.

The manager will primarily focus on four critical areas

A. Management support of grants and external engagement for vaccines program.

B. Management of internal operations for vaccines program and global vaccines team.

C. Financial management and financial system strengthening for vaccines program.

D. Management of sub team focused on finance and operations.

 Responsibilities

  • Grant Management
  • Internal Operations Management
  • Financial Management and Financial System Strengthening for Vaccines Program
  • Management of sub-team focused on program finance and operations

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree (Masters-level or above preferred) in a relevant field
  • Minimum 7 years of work experience in a fast-paced, high-performance environment; at least 4 years in related work
  • Proven team management skills
  • Excellent organizational and project management skills
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills, with mastery of excel
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with strong donor communication and writing skills
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, with ability to work effectively in a multi-cultural and often remote environment and across organizational boundaries
  • Ability to get things done, working effectively in a high-pressure environment and handling flexibly multiple tasks
  • Strong familiarity with financial systems, budgeting & management
  • Resourceful and result-oriented mindset, including ability to work independently, self-motivate, and propose and implement new initiatives
  • Rigorous and detail-oriented that can deliver very high-quality work, notably financials and written donor reports
  • Strong commitment to CHAI’s and the team’s mission and CHAI’s values, dedicated team player
  • Ability to occasionally travel internationally as needed for work

Advantages

  • Strong experience with donor communication and writing
  • Experience with developing or strengthening systems and processes, change management and operational transformation
  • Experience working with developing countries, notably in Sub-Saharan Africa
  • Professional proficiency in French
