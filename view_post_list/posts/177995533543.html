2018 親子天下教育創新國際年會
Loading
2018.09.12

這活動好像跟非洲沒關係啊，為什麼wowAfrica阿非卡會分享這個活動呢？因為我們認為：

1. 這場論壇所談論的內容符合「個人化學習」及「跨域整合」的教育趨勢。

2. 不少來自非洲朋友從過客成為臺灣的新住民，其中有不少人的下一代也將（或已經）進入台灣的教育環境，甚至有些人在臺灣擔任教職。我們認為這場活動是適合這些朋友了解、關注的議題。

3. wowAfrica阿非卡 跟多所學校進行課程合作，除了把非洲議題帶入校園，同時也不斷地探索如何將資訊更有效地傳遞給老師與同學們。

基於以上理由，我們希望更多人一起關心台灣教育的未來，所以我們決定協助宣傳《2018親子天下教育創新國際年會》。

面對快速變化的世界，從美國的史丹佛大學2025年辦學藍圖，到台灣即將上路的 108 課綱，都顯示未來的教育，將越來越看重「個人化學習」及「跨域整合」；學習的目的，不只是為了分數，而是學會用創新的方式解決問題。因此，親子天下以「適性而學，打造改變世界的人才」作為年會主題，集結兩岸三地、國內外 30 位以上重要教育創新意見領袖，推出超過10場演講、論壇、工作坊，共同探討未來教育的可能。

第一場論壇活動邀請到 矽谷學校基金執行長 布萊恩‧葛林柏（Brian Greenberg） 、 美國灣區「願景教育非營利組織Envision Education」執行長吉雅‧張（Gia Truong）上台分享， 並遠端連線 美國史丹佛大學媒體實驗室「終身幼兒園」計畫主持人 米契爾．瑞斯尼克（Mitchel Resnick） 全程將以英文進行。年會部分場次有網路直播。場次與時間請參考底下的活動訊息。

Above 30 speakers from Taiwan, China and USA will talk the future education together. You will see the innovation and new model for education. Let’s discuss how the education could be better for our next generation!

The section “Adapt to the nature and create talents that could change world” will be hold at 09:15 – 12:00 and the whole process is conducted in English. Here is the speakers’ introduction below:

Mitchel Resnick | LEGO Papert Professor of Learning Research at the MIT Media Lab

Develops new technologies and activities to engage people (particularly children) in creative learning experiences. His book, Lifelong Kindergarten, is talking how lessons from kindergarten can help everyone develop the creative thinking skills needed to thrive in today’s society.

Brian Greenberg | Board of Directors CEO, Silicon Schools Fund

Brian Greenberg is a former Chief Academic Officer, school founder, principal, and classroom teacher with organizations including Envision Schools, Leadership Public Schools Hayward, the Match school in Boston, and the Los Angeles Unified School District. A speaker nationally on personalized learning, education, and venture philanthropy, Brian is the co-creator of the Coursera and Khan Academy MOOC on blended learning, a Stanford University Principal Fellow, and an Aspen Institute Pahara Fellow. Brian received his B.A. from Dartmouth College and an M.Ed. from Harvard University.

Gia Truong | Chief Executive Officer at Envision Education

Envision Education, a mission-driven organization committed to transforming the lives of low-income, first-generation college-bound students. Gia is a Leading for Equity Fellow with the National Equity Project, where she collaborates with other leaders to foster positive change on behalf of historically under-served students and families.  She is also a member of Education Leaders of Color, an organization dedicated to elevating the leadership, voices and influence of people of color in education.
