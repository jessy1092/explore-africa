第四屆非洲互聯網論壇

非洲互聯網論壇 (IOTFA) 是非洲互聯網產業界最重要的活動，在這裡你會了解最新的互聯網應用，以及它是如何幫助企業業務和盈利增長。今年論壇談論主題包括：智慧城市、互聯網於公共安全的應用、數位轉型、區塊鏈、IT安全、人工智能、分析、互聯網戰略。

參加方式：

一、以參與者身份參加：註冊並加入本地和 international CxO、line-of-business（LoB）執行官、創新技術暨策略負責人，討論實施互聯網戰略所需的技術和工具。

二、以講者身份參加：展現你的思想與領導力，分享你的見解與經驗。

三、以參展商身份參加：展示貴公司的創新技術、項目，尋找合作夥伴、投資人、潛在客戶與機會。

論壇日期：2020/ 3/ 25-26

論壇地點：Emperors Palace, 約翰尼斯堡

更多資訊與報名：http://iotforumafrica.com

4th IOT Forum Africa 

Why attend? Internet of Things Forum Africa is the premier African event on the Internet of things. Find out how IoT can deliver unprecedented intelligence to drive performance, growth, and profitability.

Your prospects and competitors will be at IoT Forum Africa 2020.

Participate in IoT Forum Africa 2020 and showcase your technology solutions alongside Africa’s biggest IoT Players. 

Don’t miss this opportunity to engage with 500+ executives, entrepreneurs and solution providers at IOTFA 2020 in Johannesburg – for an event entirely focused on the Internet of Things. 

Key topics include Smart Cities, IoT for Public Safety, Digital Transformation, Blockchain, IT Security, AI, Analytics, IoT Strategy, IoT Adoption and so much more.

How to participate:

Join as an attendee: Register and join local and international CxOs, line-of-business (LoB) executives as well as heads of innovation, technology and strategy to discuss the techniques and tools required to implement a seamless IoT strategy that will drive profitability and efficiency.

Join as a speaker: Showcase your thought-leadership. Share your insights and experience. Shape solutions to critical business challenges.

Join us as an exhibitor: Showcase your technology innovations, projects, and solutions. Put your organisation, company or SME at the centre of this one-of-a-kind gathering. Find new partnerships, investors, leads, and opportunities.

Date：25-26 March 2020

Location：Emperors Palace, Johannesburg 

More information & Register：http://iotforumafrica.com
