【活動分享】African Banker Awards 2018
Loading
2018.02.22

We are delighted to announce the next edition of the African Banker Awards that will take place during the African Development Bank Annual Meetings in South Korea.

We are now accepting entries from banks and companies in the financial service sector.

It has been a mixed year for the banking sector as they dealt with two years of slower economic growth. Nevertheless, activity has been strong, new innovations have been rolled out, and many institutions has strengthened their appeal, evident through strong or new credit ratings. Fintech has undoubtedly dominated headlines and is changing business models. We expect another strong year of entries as banks continue to strengthen not only their balance sheets but their position within the global banking landscape.

We invite you to share with us your successes by entering and taking part in the Awards and hope to see you in May in Busan, South Korea to celebrate African excellence in banking. 

The categories this year are:

  • African Bank of the Year
  • African Banker of the Year
  • Best regional Bank in Africa
  • Investment Bank of the Year
  • Award for Financial Inclusion
  • Deal of the Year - Equity
  • Deal of the Year - Debt
  • Best Retail Bank in Africa
  • Infrastructure Deal of the Year
  • Award for Innovation in Banking
  • Socially Responsible Bank of the Year

Busan, South Korea, 22nd May 2018
